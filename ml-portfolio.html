AgentGrid — PPO Autonomous Navigation Agent

From-scratch PPO with GAE on a custom procedurally-generated Gymnasium-compatible GridWorld. 60% success rate on 10×10 grid (300k timesteps, CPU). Full hyperparameter study including entropy coefficient and clip-eps ablation. 30/30 unit tests passing.

  • PPO-Clip with GAE, shared MLP Actor-Critic (2×128, Tanh), 20,485 parameters — trained in 1,435s on CPU
  • Bimodal behaviour: successful episodes complete in exactly 14 steps; failed episodes hit 200-step timeout — maps policy sensitivity to goal position
  • 14×14 grid run (600k steps): KL spike at update 900 (0.1139) analysed as destabilising policy update with partial recovery
  • Outputs: training_curves.png, value_heatmap.png, agent_eval.gif (201-frame replay), training_log.csv
